Peptide Journal Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Peptide Journal is a local-first health journal. The app does not require an account and does not send journal data to an app-owned server.

Data You Enter

You may choose to store journal entries, user-created protocol records, symptoms, biomarkers, inventory notes, wellness notes, and export files. These entries can contain sensitive health information.

Local Storage

Journal data is stored locally on your device inside the app storage area. Peptide Journal does not provide app-owned cloud sync, advertising, analytics tracking, or third-party health data sharing.

Subscriptions

Subscriptions and trials are processed by Apple through the App Store. Apple may process purchase and subscription information under Apple's own terms and privacy policies.

Exports

Exports are created only when you choose to create, share, copy, or preview them. Once you share an export outside the app, the destination app or service controls that copy.

Medical Disclaimer

Peptide Journal is a documentation tool. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, dosage recommendations, treatment guidance, vendor recommendations, shop links, or affiliate links.
